Capstone Project

My Capstone Project is a website that will allow my mother to manage her checking account through an online check registry. Currently, my mother utilizes a paper registry where she handwrites all of her entries. I would like to create a simplistic website which will allow her to complete this task online. The site will be responsive through all viewports, so she can access the site via her handheld devices. There will be a log-in screen to ensure information is secured. Once you enter the app, you will be asked to add the account name and current balance for this account. There is an option to have multiple accounts on the site. Once the account has been setup, you will start making your entries. Each entry will include the option of placing the check number, date, transaction description, and payment/deposit. I am building the website with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap.
